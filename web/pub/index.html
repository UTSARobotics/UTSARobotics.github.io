Innovating the Future of Robotics & Automation

Mission Statement

Our Mission is to foster the development and facilitate the exchange of scientific and technological knowledge in Robotics and Automation that benefits members, the profession and humanity.

What do we do?

Workshops

Learn robotics end-to-end in guided workshops that cover design, wiring, and coding. Turn your ideas into real working prototypes while gaining hands-on experience with the full build process

Crashcourses

Robotics Crashcourses are bite-sized, 3-part learning modules designed to simplify complex robotics topics, breaking them down into practical lessons that make advanced concepts easier to understand and apply

URC

Our University Rover Challenge team is developing an advanced rover with autonomous navigation, robotic manipulation, drone integration, and automated soil testing to push the frontiers of planetary exploration.

Join our robotics society! We have a student-run lab where anyone can get help, share ideas, and work on projects. Open to all majors and experience levels!
