What are Officers?

Officers are the leaders of the organization. They are responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of the organization, as well as planning and executing events, projects, and outreach activities.

Executive Board

The executive board is responsible for the overall management and direction of the Officer Board. They oversee all aspects of the Org, including Administrative, Project management, Fundraising, and Outreach.
